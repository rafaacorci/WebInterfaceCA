Gravel bikes: For the roads untraveled

Gravel bikes are built for the less traveled roads. Equipped with drop bards, wide tyres, and versatility, they blur the line between road and off-road rioding. Making them the perfect choice from daily commutes to dirt road adventures.

A brief history

Gravel bikes were created from the growing desired to explore beyong tarmac, especially in regions rich in rugged terrains. Endless gravel roads demanded a bike tough enough to handle rough topography, but also fast enough for long distances. What once started as a niche, quickly became a movement that led to purpose built bikes with endurance geometry with off road capability.

Why buy a gravel bike?

Gravel bikes offers a degree of freedom other bikes don't. it opens up endless possibilities of mixing paved surfaces, dirt, mud and gravel roads all in one bike. Gravel bikes are stable, comfortable and with enough mounting points to for a multi-day bikepacking trip or just a weekend loop. Gravel bikes are all about exploring without the limitations.
